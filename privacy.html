Readory

Privacy Policy

Effective May 2, 2026

No personal data collected

Overview

Readory is designed to help you track your reading and reflect on your experience. Your privacy is a core principle of the app. Readory does not collect, store, or share your personal data.

Information stored on your device

Readory stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Book information, such as title, author, ISBN, and cover image
  • Reading activity, including session dates, durations, and notes
  • Personal ratings and reflections

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

External data requests

When you add a book using an ISBN, that ISBN is sent to these third-party services to request matching book data. No personal information is included in these requests.

Open Library Google Books

Analytics

Readory does not include third-party analytics or tracking frameworks.

Basic, aggregated app usage data may be provided by Apple through its App Analytics platform. This information is anonymized and does not identify individual users.

In-app purchases

Readory offers an optional in-app purchase to unlock unlimited reading sessions. All payments are processed securely through Apple's in-app purchase system. Readory does not collect or store any payment information.

On-device processing

Features such as "Summary of Notes" are performed entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence. Your notes and reading data are not sent to external servers for processing.

Data control

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete individual books and associated reading sessions at any time within the app
  • Removing the app from your device will delete all stored data

Children's privacy

Readory does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
