User Guide

Welcome
Readory is your personal reading journey tracker. It combines "reading" with "memory" to help you keep track of all the books you read (or want to read!), along with all of the associated reading session details for each book. Use the built-in reading timer to record when you read, then add details like what page you ended on and your thoughts. Over time, you'll build a long record of reading sessions to look back on. If your device supports Apple Intelligence, you can also generate a summary of your overall experience with each book. All of your data remains securely on your device and is completely private.

Library
The Library tab is where all of your books are stored and organized. Use the search button to find a specific book, or toggle between list view and category view. Additional filtering, sorting, and Settings are available behind the … button.

To add a book, tap + and scan a barcode (ISBN) to automatically retrieve book information from Open Library. If a cover isn't found, you can take a photo or upload one from your library. Tap Save when done.

Tap any book to open its detail page, where you can view session history and generate a Summary of your reading journey (on supported devices). Tap Edit to update details or delete sessions. When editing the cover, a Download option lets you search Google Books as an alternative source.

Books are grouped into Want to Read, Reading, and Finished, and update automatically — starting a session moves a book into Reading, and finishing it moves it into Finished.

Read
The Read screen is designed to keep things simple so you can focus on reading. Confirm the correct book is selected, or tap Change Book to choose a different one. Enter your starting page number and tap Start Reading — the timer begins immediately and keeps running even if you leave the app. It will also appear as a Live Activity on your Lock Screen or in the Dynamic Island once you allow it. Tap the stop button in the Live Activity or End Session in the app when you're done.

Reading Sessions
Each session represents a single block of reading time. After ending a session, you'll be prompted to confirm the duration, enter your ending page, and add a short note about what you read. If you completed the book, tap I'm Done to mark it as finished and optionally add a star rating. The book will automatically move to the Finished category.

Stats
The Stats page provides a simple summary of your reading activity over time, including book numbers, completions, and sessions.

Settings
From Settings, you can clear cached book covers or delete session data — any destructive action requires confirmation. You can also find the User Guide and Privacy Policy here. If you want to customise the AI-generated Summary of Notes, you can provide your own prompt from this screen. This section is intentionally minimal; additional options may be added over time.

Accessibility
Readory includes VoiceOver support with all key elements labeled for clear navigation. Text adjusts to your system's Dynamic Type settings, and contrast is maintained throughout the interface. These features follow your device settings automatically.

Tips
Use the app consistently, but keep each interaction light — a quick note after each session is enough to build meaningful history over time. The timer removes the need to track duration manually, so it's worth using whenever possible.

There's no need to manage book status yourself; it updates automatically. The value of the app comes from accumulation rather than precision, so focus on capturing what stands out.

The AI-generated Summary of Notes is more useful once you've built up several sessions. If you're not happy with the result, a Regenerate button will appear to try again.

If you want to try something completely different, there is an option in Settings to provide your own custom prompt for generating the summary.

Common Questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open Readory and tap Restore Purchase on the upgrade screen.

My purchase didn't unlock
Make sure you're signed into the App Store with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase, then try restoring again.

Why isn't a book cover loading?
Covers are fetched from Open Library or Google Books and may not always be available. You can use your camera or photo library to set a cover manually, or tap Download in Edit mode to try Google Books.

Do I need an account?
No. Readory does not require an account. All data is stored locally on your device.

Is my reading data private?
Yes. All data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any servers controlled by the developer. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

What is the Summary of Notes feature?
On devices that support Apple Intelligence, Readory can generate a summary of your reading experience based on the notes you've recorded across your sessions. Processing happens entirely on-device — your notes are never sent to external servers.
