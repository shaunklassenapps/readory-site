Common Questions

How do I restore my purchase?
Open Readory and tap Restore Purchase on the upgrade screen.

My purchase didn’t unlock
Make sure you're signed into the App Store, then try restoring your purchase again.

Why isn’t a book cover loading?
Covers are fetched from online sources and may not always be available.

Do I need an account?
No. Readory does not require an account.
