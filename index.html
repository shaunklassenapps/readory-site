Features

Everything your reading life needs.

Reading Log
Track every book you've read, are reading, or want to read. Add notes, page numbers, and reflections to each session as you go.
Built-in Reading Timer
Start the timer when you open your book. When you're done, log your ending page and thoughts — Readory handles the rest.
Session History
Every session is saved with its timestamp, duration, page range, and your notes. Scroll back through your journey with any book.
Reading Journey Summary
On supported devices, Apple Intelligence can generate a thoughtful summary of your overall experience with any book — synthesised from all your session notes.
Reading Stats
See your total sessions, time read, and books completed. A quiet record of your reading habit, growing over time.
Completely Private
No account required, no data collection, and no analytics — not even anonymised. Your reading life is yours alone.
Your reading data is never collected or shared
Privacy

No account. No cloud. No tracking.

Readory was built with privacy as a foundation, not an afterthought. Everything you log — your books, your sessions, your thoughts — is yours. We don't collect it, we don't analyse it, and we don't share it.

There are no user accounts, no data collection of any kind, and no analytics — not even anonymised ones.

Private by design
